Text Preprocessing:
====================

Two types:

1.Text Cleaning
===================

   **Before Cleaning** - "I can't believe ho% &w many people"
   **After Cleaning**  -   "I cant believe how many people"

  - Removes text noise like punctation, slangs(etc,bro,swag)
  - spell mistakes
  - text encoding & cast fixings
  - like text encoding preferred - **big5** for **chinese**, **ascii** for **english**
  - change ase e.g "**Cap**" to **"cap"** for uniformity.
