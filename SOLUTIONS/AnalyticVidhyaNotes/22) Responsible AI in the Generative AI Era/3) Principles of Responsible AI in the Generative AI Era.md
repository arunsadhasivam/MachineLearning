Principles of RAI:
===================

    - this means, adhering to the set of principles that revolve around fairness, transparency,accountability,
      robustness, stability, privacy and security.


1.fairness:
============

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f6ee018e-fd8c-4adc-871f-76b121657998)

    - bias can creep through ai systems by various avenues , the most common of which is training data.
    - if the data used to train the genAI model is skewed or contain unfair patterns, the model may unwittingly 
      learn and perpetuate these bias in a generative output.
    - morever algorithmic design choices such as feature selections or optimizations objective can inadvertentlty
      favour certain groups or attributes over other leading to biased outcomes.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e02550c1-c1b8-461f-84fd-f30174b4c3fb)

    - feedback loops and reinforcement mechanisms where ai models learn from user interactions may also reinforce 
      existing biases.


Diversity & inclusivity in genAI:
=================================

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e16a197d-83d0-4f00-a4b9-73d409c1411a)

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8215f9f5-4b0e-4fac-80d7-a5a6a8ca88ac)

 - Additional factor to consider is the diversity and inclusivity of the teams building these products.
 - lack of this can lead to AI systems perceptuating the societal sterotypes.
 - to address these issues , it is crucial to promote diverse and representative training data.
 - establish clear guidelines on algorithms design , incorporate fairness metrics during model evaluation.
 - we must encourage diverse and inclusive development teams, and continuously monitor and evaluate the
   generative ai systems for risky behaviour.

2.transparency:
===============
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/277ca8ad-313e-4c47-a98a-eb6bcbfb738b)
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2789fc2b-92dc-483e-93b5-b4b828d0127f)
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/470f0759-f954-449c-bd63-b1ee6407c59b)

  - transparency is equally important.
  - these ai systems operate through complex algorithms , such as neural networks that are used to
    generate various forms of content such as images, text and even music.
  - however, the underlying mechanism governing how these output are generated are intricate and
    challenging to explain.
  - this lack of transparency stems from the model being training on the vast amount of data
    which can introduce biases and intricate relationships that are hard to decipher.
  - for e.g GPT-3 was trained on 45 terabytes of data and had 175 billion parameters understanding how these
    biases are captured and propagated with in the models output is a challenge that requires innovative
    solutions.

3.Accountability:
===================
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/08996ae0-5f9e-4f8b-9254-d1cecd856fbd)

  - multifaceted concept.
  - it necessitates the instution of explainablity to make the decision making process transparent to stake holders.
  - this means understanding not only how the model arrives at the decision , but also the factors that influencing these
    decisions and identifying the potential biases or limitations.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b06c3e5a-f4c5-4f8a-85d3-ec53b2051d2a)

  - Accountability principles encompasses a range of factor including responsibility for outcomes, monitoring and
    auditing the model performs and output, and regulatory complaint and user empowerment.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f0f77f4f-c74f-4845-b908-6d2b316b83f1)

  - Establishing clear documentation of the datasource,model architecture, training procedure is **vital to holding
    relevant stake holders accountable for outcomes generated by these models**.

4.Robustness and stability:
===========================
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c69b6d63-f47c-44f3-b337-c42e0bcd2808)

  - critical aspect to consider
  - consistent output for the prompt.
  - however they often struggle with these consistency
  - particularly when dealing with complex and diverse datasets.
  - maintaining **consistent patterns and distributions across samples can be challening.**
  - unrealiable genAI models can produces output that are  unrealistic, implausible or failed to
    capture the underlying distribution thus limiting the practical usefullness.
  - ensure the robustness and stability in genAI requires ongoing researh and devlopment efforts to
    address these issues and enhance the overall performance and reliability.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f3e2fc47-976d-4eb7-b9bf-bcbe12f1a598)

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e43e3102-732a-4164-b2ee-88a191790a09)

  - to ensure the consistency and reliability of the genai , rigorous quality control measures must be
    established throughout the pipeline . this involves thorough testing , validation and evaluation
    of the genAI output against predefined criteria and benchmark.
  - comprehensive testing is crucial to access the robustness of genAI systems against adversial attacks,
    input variations, and edge cases ultimately improving their reliability.
